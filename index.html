Gabe Newell

Co-founder of Valve and Visionary behind Steam

The man who changed PC gaming forever!

A tribute to Gabe Newell

Gabe Newell is more than just a video game developer, he is a legend who democratized gaming. Leaving Microsoft in 1996 to co-found Valve with Harrington Mike, Gabe helped create some of the most influential Game titles in history. Most of which are still played today. His relentless pursuit for quality, innovation and player freedom led to the creation of Steam, the world's largest distribution platform for video games and other content.

Known as Gaben in the gaming community, his humor, humility and passion for technology continues to inspire millions of people.
